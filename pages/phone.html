Plain talk: PlowzBox does not answer calls with a robot voice. It rescues the calls you miss, by text, where most customers would rather be anyway.

Missed-call rescue

From ring-out to reply, without you touching a thing.

Three things happen the moment a call goes unanswered. Only the last one waits for you.

  1. The caller gets a text right away

    An automatic text goes out from your business number: sorry we missed you, text us here and we will get right back to you. It lands while they are still holding the phone, before they scroll to the next company on Google.

  2. The voicemail becomes text

    If they leave a voicemail, it is transcribed on the box itself. The audio never goes to a cloud service. Nobody's server hears your customers.

  3. A reply is drafted and waits for you

    The assistant reads the transcript, checks your price book and your real calendar, and drafts the reply. It sits in your queue until you approve it with one tap.

After-hours coverage

Nights and weekends stop leaking jobs.

The text-back goes out at 9 PM on a Saturday exactly like it does at 9 AM on a Tuesday. The caller hears from your business in seconds, even when your business is asleep.

What it is, plainly

Text-first. On purpose.

Some products lead with a synthetic voice on your phone line. PlowzBox leads with the rescue that works every time: the text-back. An after-hours phone agent that answers and books is part of early access as it rolls out, and anything it can't handle goes straight to you. We would rather tell you that plainly here than have you find out at the demo.

What it does

Rescues the call by text

  • Texts every missed caller back in seconds, from your business number.
  • Transcribes voicemail locally, on the box, no cloud audio.
  • Drafts a grounded text reply from your price book and calendar.
  • Covers nights and weekends without you lifting a finger.
What it does not do

Talk to your customers out loud

  • No robot voice picks up your line or holds a spoken conversation.
  • No phone-tree menus, no “press 2 for scheduling.”
  • It never pretends a text-back is a live answering service.

Why we built it this way: a fast, honest text with a real answer beats a stilted robot reading a script. That is why the rescue leads with text, and why the after-hours phone agent joins only where it can answer well and hand the rest to you. Your customers keep talking to you. The box just makes sure the conversation starts.
