Payment reminders

It drafts the nudge. You send it.

Chasing money from good customers is the worst chore in the business. The assistant writes the firm, polite version so you don't have to find the words. You just decide whether today's the day.

  • The reminder is drafted with the invoice and your pay link included, and it waits in your queue.
  • Every payment reminder requires your explicit one-tap approval. Every time. That is a hard rule, not a setting.
  • The tone is yours to edit. Firm, friendly, or “third notice” formal.

Straight talk about money

Three things this page will not oversell.

Fact 01

Not a payment processor

PlowzBox does not hold, move, or touch money. Customers pay through the pay link you already use, whatever bank or payment service that is. Your money never routes through our box.

Fact 02

Never claims money moved

The assistant never tells a customer a payment went through, and never tells you one did, unless you recorded it. No wishful bookkeeping.

Fact 03

Reminders are always gated

Every payment reminder waits for your one-tap approval before it sends. This one is a hard rule in the software, not a preference you could fat-finger off.
